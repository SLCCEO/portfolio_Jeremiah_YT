SLC-12: Wraith Glacier

Installation Origin • Class-4 Cryo-Research & Training Zone

Located beneath an ancient glacier, SLC-12 is the original foundation of Singularity Labs Corporation. It is not only a containment site but also a hub for simulation, training, and early recruitment. All entities in and around the site are uncontained, actively wandering within quarantined sectors or open terrain. The facility represents the first fully operational zone under SLC ownership following the collapse of SCP foundation.

Key Features:

  • Simulation Training Chambers for Tactical and CQC readiness
  • On-site recruitment through SLC-12 Programs (Civilians may explore or join)
  • Wandering Entities active in surrounding quarantine sectors
  • Exterior Civilian Spawn Zones with open exploration access
  • Active quarantine zones for anomaly breach containment drills

Plans & Future Expansion:

Future developments at SLC-12 will expand its civilian onboarding pipeline, improve outdoor anomaly containment perimeters, and establish layered defense zones around the glacier. It remains a high-traffic onboarding site for new recruits and researchers alike.

SLC-09: Iron Warden

Central Command Node • Advanced AI Research Hub

Serving as the nerve center of Singularity Labs, SLC-09 is the home of the CASIA AI. Built with security in mind, this facility was responsible for the containment of over 200 anomalies, secure intelligence routing, and experimental ethics-driven AI governance.

Key Features:

  • CASIA: Command and Support Integrated Assistant
  • Multi-tier security clearance system
